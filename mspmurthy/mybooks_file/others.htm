I have read your autobiography-“gere datida mele”.

My comments:

  • It is very lucid and page turner in all aspects.
  • It has got the simplicity of first time writer. It is really good, which brings authenticity to the work, what can I say `straight from the heart.’
  • Sometimes while writing an autobiography people tend to glorify their success/failures. I did not see any such glorification in this work. More importantly there is no self pity.
  • Best part which I liked in the book was you never put blame on anybody else.
  • The autobiography is completely personal and that is how it should be.
  • Finally why I liked the book is because I can relate to the book at least 40% to myself!!!

Faults:

  • I did not find any.
